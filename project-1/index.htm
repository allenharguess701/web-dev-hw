Originally Written on December 2nd 1998
Published in "An Hour At Sunrise" © 2000
Under the original title: Day Dream Garden

Author: Allen Harguess

Suddenly at this moment I close my eyes.
A thought of you grows inside my mind.
Writing this is well worth the effort. I forget myself.

I think of the rose you asked for. Its image fills my mind.
I hope I never wake. A daydream of roses; I've come to see that's you.

The day dreams on -

A sunny day Not a cloud in the sky.
Wind softly blowing. Leaves passing me bye.
Under a bridge; dark, then back into light.
The day still dreaming bye, passing into the night -

Row upon row of roses. You stand out in the crowd.

Please let me live this daydream a little while longer.

I see the truth upon the water.
This place for me to rest and learn and grow.
I see the truth you are the flower.
Standing where the peaceful waters flow.

My faith is strengthened by you don't you see.
You make it easy for me to believe.

A rose in the moonlight in my daydream garden.
