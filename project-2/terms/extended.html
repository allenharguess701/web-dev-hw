Extended & Endgame

Now on to the last two terms. In dealing with extended or end game situations you will follow much the same path as before save a few important things. You will absolutely need to have more developed and redundant systems and procedures in place and working. renewable resources must be developed and expanded. Law and Order will be what you make it. Security will be paramount. And last but certainly not least you are going to want to link up with other survivors. Building a new civilization will take numbers and skills; lots of both, many of which you will not have. Much of this column is set in relation to these two terms. Honestly all you're going to do is expand your long term SM. Your goals will be much different but the means to getting to then will be very similar. Nothing really fully prepares you for such a happenstance. All you can do is use your skills and knowledge to you best advantage. In these terms those who have knowledge truely will have power. So hopefully you have a good understanding of SCOPE. Everything I touched on was in general terms. Ideas; food for thought. In later installments I will go into much greater detail and give you specific methods for survival. The thing to take away from understanding SCOPE and TERMS is that you will always start with a short-term SM and simply build upon it as you plan to survive the successive, longer terms. So, looking back on the life cycle of a disaster; attention to the Prodromal stage is important. When it hits, it will give you a good idea of what to expect from the latter stages. The Acute stage should confirm your expectation or present you with the true reality you face. Living through the Intermediate stage will be your first real hurdle to overcome. That's where your prepping and planning will shine.
