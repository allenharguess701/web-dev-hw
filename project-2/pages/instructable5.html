Wrapping this installment up-

So-

With what I have outlined in the previous pages of this article, I have created a fairly basic yet comprehensive framework from which you can build an SM for any situation. Using these ideas you can keep even the most elaborate plan straight and easy to understand for everyone within your group.

For that is the cornerstone to any SM. Each and every member of your group, right down to the young children, must be able to implement or utilize any part of it. Think of it this way. If you're the keystone of your group; you have all the skills, did all the prepping, made all of the plans, and you get taken out; what happens to the rest of your group. Darwinism always think Darwinism. Also keep in mind that much of what I have stressed is subjective yet much can be specific to the Term of a given scenario.
