Building your SM's. an overview by Terms

Short Term: 3 to 8 weeks.
Long Term: 3 to 6 Months.
Extended Term: More than a cycle of the four seasons.
End Game: There is no hope of returning to the state of living before the event, at least not in the lifetime of those who witnessed the onset of the disaster and survived.

So let's start with short term and work our way through to end game. For the most part each successive Term will cause an addition to the SM you build. Meaning; the SM for a short-term disaster will have additions place within it when considering and building your long term SM; likewise, the long term will have further additions when building an extended SM, and so on. Really it's just one big SM, but differentiating between them really is essential. You wouldn't enact plans in a short-term disaster designed for a long term plan; you'd most likely waist time and resources.

In fact all you're really doing, when you're building a successively long term SM is adding to the shorter term plans and possibly making some minor modifications. Remember, even though you put SM's together it is your ability to adapt and mitigate your survival needs through your knowldge and hands on skills that will win you the day. The SM's a just a framework to effectively employ that knowledge and skills.
