THE 24 RULES OF SURVIVAL:

Twenty-four basic rules every survivor should live by in their daily struggle to prep or survive. Yes, I know your first thought is of Zombieland and while I will admit I did get the idea from the film, I went that direction purely as a mode to stress the importance of keeping those specific do's and don'ts in the forefront of your mind. It was a cool movie by the way.

The thing to take away from this list isn't just the content but the idea of developing a routine and sticking to it. Consistance is a key to life and it is most certainly a key in your success at surviving.
  • 1. Know when to make the hard decisions.
  • 2. Assume Nothing
  • 3. Take No Unnecessary Risks
  • 4. Take Advantage of Every Opportunity
  • 5. Keep It Simple
  • 6. Stick With What Works
  • 7. Stay Clean. Stay Dry.
  • 8. Don't Be Lazy
  • 9. Waist Nothing
  • 10. Don't Lose Your Head
  • 11. Stealth, use it!
  • 12. Organize, live it!
  • 13. Division of Labor, love it!
  • 14. Always Plan Ahead
  • 15. Keep The Faith
  • 16. Train & Educate
  • 17. Be Hands-On with everything
  • 18. Stay Informed
  • 19. Know Your Limitation
  • 20. Safety First, Always
  • 21. Secrecy First, Always
  • 22. Travel Bugout Style
  • 23. The Buddy System
  • 24. Fight Fast. Fight to Kill.
