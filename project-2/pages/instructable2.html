FACTORS:

The major situations, events, and statistics of a survival situation that will directly or indirectly affect your SM. There are six primary and six secondary factors. They are listed by Weight.

CONCEPTS:

Concepts are strict areas that any survivor should focus their SM's on. You can also describe concepts as assets, strengths, or abilities that a survivor can improve through by direct action.

Primary Factors

  • Scope
  • Climate and Surrounding
  • Type of Disaster
  • Initial Survival Rate
  • The number & status of your group
  • Clear and Present Danger

Secondary Factors

  • Available Communication
  • Natural Resources
  • Decision: Bugout or Hunker Down
  • Government Reactions
  • Civil Reactions
  • Are Zombies actually involved?

Concepts

  • Strength of Resolve
  • Adaptability
  • Accountability
  • Buddy Checks
  • Situational Awareness
  • Clear and Present Danger
  • Mad Survival Skills

    2. Hunting
    3. Gathering & Cultivation
    4. >Building & Technical
    5. Scientific & Medical
