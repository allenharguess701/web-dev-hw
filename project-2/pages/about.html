About

The Lost and Found is a part of the Harguess.com Network. A group of sites dedicated to various pursuits. Here at the LaF we use zombies and the idea of the apocalypse as a metaphor giving us a fun way to teach people of the importance of being self reliant, prepared and educated in how to survive any circumstance thrown at them. Together with written formats, audio, and video; we will convey various topics of study. While much of this site is geared towards entertainment most of the content will have real world applications in the areas of prepping and survival. Above all else it is the goal of the LaF to teach users how to survive.
